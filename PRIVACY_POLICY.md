Who Wins ("the App") is a mobile application that helps you stay accountable with friends through challenges.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1. Account Information
When you create an account:
- Email address  
- Username (display name)
- Profile photo (optional)

### 1.2. Photos
When you perform a check-in:
- You take or upload a photo as proof
- Photos are stored securely on our servers
- **EXIF metadata** (GPS coordinates, camera model, etc.) is **automatically removed** for your privacy
- Only participants in your challenge can see your check-in photos

### 1.3. Location Data
**GPS coordinates** are collected **ONLY** when you tap the check-in button:
- Used to verify you are at the challenge location (geofencing)
- Collected in **foreground only** (never in background)
- We **DO NOT** track your location at any other time
- Location permission is requested only when needed

### 1.4. Payment Information
All payments are processed by **Stripe** (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified):
- We **DO NOT** store your full card details on our servers
- We only store a Stripe customer ID and payment method ID
- Stripe handles all credit/debit card information securely
- Used for challenge stakes and withdrawals

### 1.5. App Usage Data
We collect:
- Challenges you join or create  
- Check-ins and progress  
- App interactions (screens visited, features used)

### 1.6. Device Information
We may collect:
- Device model  
- Operating system version  
- App version
- Crash logs (to fix bugs)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide the App:** Create challenges, verify check-ins, process payments
- **Improve the App:** Analyze usage to enhance features and fix bugs
- **Security:** Prevent cheating, detect fraud, ensure fair results
- **Communicate:** Send check-in reminders, account updates, support responses

We **NEVER** sell your data to third parties.

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1. With Other Users
Within a challenge, participants can see:
- Your username and profile photo
- Your check-in status (pass/fail) and photos
- Your challenge history for that specific challenge

### 3.2. With Service Providers
We share data with:
- **Supabase** (database and authentication)
- **Stripe** (payment processing)
- **Expo** (push notifications)

These providers only process data on our behalf and are bound by strict data protection agreements.

### 3.3. Legal Obligations
We may disclose information if required by law (court orders, legal requests).

We **never** share your data with advertisers or for marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- All data is transmitted over **HTTPS/TLS** encryption
- Passwords are **hashed** using industry-standard bcrypt
- Payment data is handled by **Stripe** (PCI-DSS Level 1)
- Photos are stored in a private bucket with restricted access
- **EXIF metadata** is removed from all photos to protect your privacy

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** Data is kept as long as your account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** All data is permanently deleted within **30 days**
- **Legal requirements:** Some financial records may be retained longer for tax/compliance purposes
